El Salvador’s economy relies heavily on the coffee industry. However, due to corruption and bribery, there has been a rise in unpaid labor. In order to combat this, ChainBytes has teamed up with Lehigh University in order to develop an application that utilizes blockchain technology to uphold Fair Trade standards in the coffee industry. Our goal is to make the payment process of workers more transparent, so corporations, as well as customers, can buy coffee without worrying about its integrity.
